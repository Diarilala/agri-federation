# 🌾 Agri-Federation 🌾

> **Digitalizing the administrative processes of the National Federation of Agricultural Communities of Madagascar**

---

## 📖 Overview

The **Federation of Agricultural Communities** (*Fédération des Collectivités Agricoles*) is a national-level organization established in Madagascar to amplify the voice of Malagasy farmers. Its mission is to usher in a new era for the agricultural sector by connecting all stakeholders — from farmers to exporters.

What began as a single community has grown into a nationwide movement, now counting **400+ registered members** across **10+ cities** throughout Madagascar. To sustain this rapid growth and ensure operational continuity, the federation is digitalizing its administrative processes through this platform.
